      \n Note\n

      \n
        \n
      • % change is calculated with respect to close price of normal market. Close price is adjusted price on\n ex-date for Dividend, Bonus, Rights & Face Value Split.
        • \n
      • Adjusted Price: Adjusted price is displayed when the security is undergoing a Corporate Action such\n as Dividend, Bonus, Rights or Face Value Split. On Corporate Action (Ex-date), the %change on is calculated\n with respect to the Adjusted Price.
        • \n
      • i-NAV : i-NAV (Indicative NAV) for an ETF is displayed as received from AMCs during each business\n day.
        • \n
      • For securities that undergo call auction in special pre-open session in case of New/IPO Listings;\n Re-Listing; Corporate Action; Surveillance action etc., the % change is calculated with respect to\n equilibrium price determined in the session.
        • \n
      • Close price will be updated after 18.15 hrs.
        • \n
      • 52 week High/Low: Prices are adjusted for Corporate Action such as Bonus, Face Value Split, Rights etc.
        • \n
      • The dynamic price bands of 10% applicable, for securities in derivatives or securities included in indices\n on which derivative products are available, may be relaxed intra-day in the event of a market trend in\n either direction (upper/lower) in co-ordination with other Exchanges.
        • \n
      • Daily and Annualised volatility is as of previous day EOD based on EWMA methodology as used for margin\n computation.
        • \n
      • 52 week high & 52 week low prices are historically not adjusted for Bonus, Split & Rights Corporate\n actions.
        • \n
      \n \n

      For P/E: 

      \n \n
        \n
      • Standalone Financial Results will be considered if consolidated is not applicable for any listed company.\n
        • \n
      • For the computation of net profit, the financial results of last 4 consecutive quarters for Equity companies\n and 2 consecutive half year ended for SME companies is considered.
        • \n
      • In the case of new listings/scheme of arrangements/migration to Main board \xe2\x80\x93 Computation shall be applicable\n when the data for last 4 quarters is available.
        • \n
      • "Adjusted P/E" is after adjustments of Exceptional items (net of tax), if any.
